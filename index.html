Visualization Modes

  • Bitmap: Renders each byte as a grayscale pixel.
  • Byte Density: Highlights frequently occurring bytes in red and less common ones in green.
  • Entropy: Displays data randomness: high entropy (random) in blue, low entropy (structured) in red.
  • ASCII Map: Colors different ASCII character types: printable (green), control (blue), extended (red), and null (black).
